volunteer

We require people who have know-how, some initiative and lot of enthusiasm to get involved with teaching arts, sports, educational support(Holistic approach), skill development and crafts to underprivileged children.This program aims to bring out the skills and talents of unprivileged children. We are looking for volunteers to take up this rigorous and challenging opportunity to develop themselves as leaders and simultaneously transform the life of children through our program.We also require people to undertake our various events,campaigns,workshop,drives and sales.

What You have to do?

We believe that to work for social change you need to contribute a substantial amount of time to understand the needs and cater in the best possible way with your unique skill set. This movement to end social inequity, provide better education and bring out the skills and talents of unprivileged children is a long journey and we cannot do it our own. If you are passionate about our vision and have a certain number of hours in a week to put in, you can volunteer with us.We are currently present in Kerala and Bangalore.
