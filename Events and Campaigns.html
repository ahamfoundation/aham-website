Events and Campaigns


This project provides a platform for young people who would like to work for various campaigns and events of AHAM FOUNDATION. Our volunteers work for various campaigns, fund raising events and promotions. This includes campaigning for social relevant issues to organizing birthday celebrations. One of our major campaign is ‘Saviors Of Life-Organ Donation Awareness Campaign’.

How it works?

Our volunteers organize ‘Saviors of Life-Organ Donation Awareness Campaign’ in various parts of Kerala and Bangalore associating with residents associations, clubs and organizations which can keep a follow up to make results. Awareness using social media is another approach.


Our volunteers organize various events to birthday celebrations with the children and underprivileged in various destitute and shelter homes. Our team organizes events quickly and efficiently saving your time and energy and makes it worth.

Students and professionals who volunteer with Events and campaign are given training sessions which covers all aspects of how to conduct events and campaigns. The team meets every month to plan out and execute events depending on the demography of the city.

IMPACT

The project ‘Events and Campaigns’ is designed in such a way to extend its activities to all the sections of society and motivate more people to become the change makers. This has been capable to provide immense support on its on-ground social projects and increase the reach. This would help in the uplifment of underprivileged and bring back the selflessness in people.

 

 

We believe that to work for social change you need to contribute a substantial amount of time to understand the needs and cater in the best possible way with your unique skill set.
