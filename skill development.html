SKILL DEVELOPMENT


To face the competitions in the real world children are equipped through different skill development classes taken by experts and leaders from different sector. This ranges from communication and technical skill development to leadership training.

How it works?

Professionals and experts conduct skill development classes regularly for the children in orphanages and shelter homes. The course is standardized and ensures that children have all the required skill sets to thrive in competitive world.

IMPACT
This provides a platform for professionals and experts to come together and transform the lives of children and India’s label from ‘Scammed India’ to ‘Skilled India’

We believe that to work for social change you need to contribute a substantial amount of time to understand the needs and cater in the best possible way with your unique skill set.
