Holistic Education & Support


AHAM FOUNDATION works with children live in orphanages, shelter homes who have very less opportunity to learn and lack guidance to meet the challenges in life as well as in academics. The value of knowing thyself and relationships is given prime importance in this since the institutions created for children's learning have little time nor resources.
This Program seeks to work towards providing our children with teachers and counselors who would help them perform better at schools as well as in daily life. This helps them to realize their true strengths and become successful in life.

How it works?

Holistic Education and Educational Support project deals with academic as well as overall development of children
This Program will be providing a set of children with volunteers who would provide them with after school support in subjects such as Mathematics, Science and English following the school curriculum of the children. The teachers will be provided with mentors, training modules to help them to do their best. This program will cater to grades 3 to 10.
By pairing with an excellent counselor we will enable children to realize their true strengths, passion and unleash their potential. As a trusted advisor, guide and much like an elder brother or sister he/she will discuss with children and try to resolve the problems. Special sessions will be given to the children to learn about themselves, relationships, social and emotional development

IMPACT
The impact of the holistic education and educational support is gauged by regular assessments internally.

 

 

We believe that to work for social change you need to contribute a substantial amount of time to understand the needs and cater in the best possible way with your unique skill set.
