ARTS & SPORTS TRAINING


Do poor children have as much a right to play and enjoy as rich ones do? Do they get opportunities to come up as rich ones do? The aim of this project is to answer this by bringing out the talents of underprivileged children by providing training in arts and sports and take them to the world of opportunities. We are committed to find, develop and support Indiaâ€™s brightest and most promising athletes, artists, sportsmen from underprivileged children who miss the opportunities due to the socio- economic background.

How it works?
AHAM FOUNDATION associate with reputed organizations and experts for arts and sports training of underprivileged children. The classes will be taken based on a syllabus formulated and tested by a panel of experts. Talented children are found after a series of classes, selection trials and talent hunts. The selected children will be given best training and venues to show their talents.
IMPACT
This project aims to find and develop the most promising talents from underprivileged. They may be the future talents who make our country proud. This ensures that arts and sports training is for all children and socio-economic background is not a hurdle.

 

We believe that to work for social change you need to contribute a substantial amount of time to understand the needs and cater in the best possible way with your unique skill set.
